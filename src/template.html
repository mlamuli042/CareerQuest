The Career Quest is adopted from material prepared by officers from the Educational, Testing, Guidance and Psychological Services (ETGPS) in the Ministry Of Educational and Training (MOET). Career Interest was revised and field tested in Eswatini schools and with Emaswati students in order to make the inventory more suitable for the Kingdom of Eswatini.

We express our gratitude for the extensive work performed to support in career guidance, further training, scholarship selection, and workforce development in country.
